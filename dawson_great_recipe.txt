set oven to 350 degrees farenheit

melt 12 oz of semisweet chocolate in microwave

put 4 egg whites and one cup sugar in stand mixer and mix until stiff
peaks form.

add in cooled, melted chocolate, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla, add 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
and mix thoroughly

put spoonful of chocolate cookie batter onto baking sheets, and bake for 10-15
minutes or until toothpick comes out clean from cookies.

enjoy
